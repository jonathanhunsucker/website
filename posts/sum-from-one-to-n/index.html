Visual understanding of sum from 1 to n

On May 12, 2019 by Jonathan Hunsucker

The sum from one to n can be clarified visually. Below is a rectangle that’s n+1 cells wide and n cells tall. Half the cells are shaded in, representing the quantityn*(n+1)/2. As n grows by one, another row and colum is introduced, with the entire new column being shaded (accounting for the increment).
